December 5th

It was a quiet night.

You called to order food, just like you had before.

I already knew your name: Aswani.

But that night, the delivery boy was busy.

So I decided to deliver the food myself.

I got in my car and started driving...

listening to your voice on the phone,

giving me instructions how to reach.

🚗

I parked outside the building.

Then... you came down from your mother's room.

Walking towards me in the dim light,

standing there in your pink churidar.

The world didn't stop.

It just… became quieter.

Everyone around me faded away.

The noise became silence.

And all I could see…

was you.

I thought I was delivering food...

I didn't know I was delivering my heart.

We had never met before that day…

but somehow, my heart already recognised you.

As if we had known each other

in another lifetime.

You already felt like home.

Even though I only knew your name from a delivery order.

It started with food.

A simple question.

A simple answer.

But something made me come back.

The next day…

and the next…

and the next.
